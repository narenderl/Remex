About

Welcome to Lansing Square Condos (LSQ Condos), a keenly-awaited mixed-use residential development by Almadev in North York. Ideally located in the Sheppard East community, it will be a visible focal point attracting single professionals, business owners, families and others in one central location. LSQ will be prominent in the Sheppard East community with its spectacular eye-catching architecture. Once inside, you will be greeted by well-laid-out residential units with spacious interiors and all the modern amenities your heart desires.
